Integrate the Free Online Reservation System from Campsite Reports into your WordPress site. Easily accept online reservations for your campground.

== Description ==

The Campsite Reports online reservation system allows you to accept online reservations for your campground with minimal effort!

This plugin requires that you have an account (free) with [Campsite Reports](http://www.campsitereports.com) for your campground.
Don't have a Campsite Reports account?  [Qucikly create an account for your campground here.](http://www.campsitereports.com/htm/free-campground-reservation-system.php)

* Accept reservations on your WordPress website
* This system is free for campground owners to use (a small service fee is charged to the customer)
* Quick and easy setup and integration

== Installation ==

1. Upload the folder 'campsitereports to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
1. Activate the plugin 'Campsite Reports OFFICIAL Wordpress Plugin' through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place shortcode in your page in your pages as described on the 'CSR Settings -> Instructions' page in the WordPress Admin
